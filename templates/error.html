Our Model Can Detect Only These Diseases

Rhizoctonia seedling blight

A soilborne fungus that causes seedling blight in a variety of crops. Symptoms include reddish-brown lesions on the hypocotyls at the soil line.

Pythium Chlorotic disease

A water mold that causes chlorosis (yellowing) in a variety of crops. Symptoms include stunted growth, yellowing leaves, and wilting.

Penicillium Leaf curling

A fungus that causes leaf curling in a variety of crops. Symptoms include curled leaves, stunted growth, and reduced yields.
